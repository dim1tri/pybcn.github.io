PyDataBCN 2017

PyData has arrived to Barcelona!

The PyDataBCN 2017 will take place from May 19th to 21th at ESADE. Save the date!

PyData brings together both users and developers of data analysis tools, from Python, R, Julia and others, to share ideas and learn from each other. The goal is to provide Data Science enthusiasts across various domains a place to discuss how to best apply languages and tools to the challenges of data management, processing, analytics, and visualisation.

Registration will open on March 20th.

The Call for Proposals is open!

Do you love data analysis and beautiful visualizations? Do you use Python, R or Julia and want to share your experience with others? Are you working on an interesting data science project? Do you want to recommend your best practices and share your technical tips and tricks? Have you integrated new solutions and technologies?

Join us! Propose either a talk or a workshop.

The deadline is on April 9th.

We’re looking for Sponsors!

Sponsoring a PyData event has many benefits:

  • Recruiting: PyData's attendees include data scientists, developers, software engineers, data analysts, researchers, product architects, postdocs and graduate students. PyData is the place to find top data professionals.
  • Marketing: PyData is a great place to demo your products and services. Your exhibition space or sponsor talk provides an excellent platform to educate industry experts on your latest tools and to receive valuable feedback.
  • Community and Diversity: Your support enables diversity outreach as well as financial assistance and scholarships to many who would otherwise be unable to attend.
